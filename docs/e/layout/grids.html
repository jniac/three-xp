css-like margins:

  • 4 values (10, 30, 90, 0) (top, right, bottom, left)
  • 2 values (10, 80) (vertical, horizontal)
  • specific overrides: marginLeft overrides "2 values" declaration

Margins "collapse" when they are adjacent to each other or to the edge (padding) of the container.

margin: 0

margin: 40

margin: 120

alignSelf allows the children to override the parent's alignChildren.

When positioning is set to detached, spaces are not affected by their parent's size.
